What is Netplay?

Well, Netplay is multiplayer gameplay over a network.

So you can play your favorite games online with your friends.

How to play?

  1. Go to any game with "NP" on it.
  2. Go to the Netplay button
  3. Type in your username
  4. Make a room
  5. Ask your friends to join your room
  6. WALLAH, YOU DID IT!
